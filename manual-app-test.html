Common errors to look for:

  • "Cannot access uninitialized variable" - This means our fix didn't work
  • "Failed to fetch" - Network/database connection issue
  • "Invalid login credentials" - Authentication issue
  • "Table 'orders' doesn't exist" - Database schema issue
  • Orders don't appear after creation - Persistence issue
  • Orders disappear after refresh - Cache/state issue
